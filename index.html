Key Features

Everything you need to manage logistics operations

Operations Console

Full console to manage all of your packages and custom systems.

TMS

End to end transport management system for logistics operations management and overview.

Driver App

Open source driver app integrated into your TMS. Drivers can manage jobs, tracking, and electronic POD.

Developer API

Easy to use developer console which provides API usage metrics, and never miss a beat with event and request logs.

Fleet Management

Manage all of your assets in one centralised location. Quickly assign assets to custom zones, service areas, and orders.

Integrations

Extensive list of integrations to get you started quickly. All the way from Lala Move to Shopify. Easy to integrate API.

Fuel Tracking

Monitor fuel consumption and track fuel expenses effectively.

Parking Monitoring

Keep track of parking spaces and monitor parking expenses in real-time.

Toll Expense Record

Record and manage toll expenses efficiently.

Vehicle Issue Tracking

Track and resolve vehicle issues for seamless operations.

Customisable

Adapt this open source solution to fit your specific business needs.

Multi-lingual

Support for multiple languages to cater to a global audience.
