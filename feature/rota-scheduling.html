Rota Scheduling & Planning

Build flexible weekly schedules that respect driver availability, compliance rules, and operational needs.

Book a Demo

Key Benefits

Build weekly rotas 10x faster with visual grid and keyboard shortcuts-eliminate spreadsheet chaos

Click to mark working days, rest days, holidays. Swap drivers in seconds. Multi-week rotas scheduled in under 30 minutes instead of 4+ hours.

Eliminate driver confusion and last-minute absences with clear, accessible schedules

Drivers see their rota on mobile instantly. No paper manifests. No missed updates. Absences drop 30% when drivers know schedule clearly.

Reduce driver burnout and turnover by fairly distributing workload across your team

Workload analytics show who's overloaded and who has capacity. Prevent exhaustion. Fair distribution improves retention by 20%.

Scale scheduling from 50 to 500+ drivers without adding admin staff

Bulk-import pre-planned rotas in under 2 minutes. Map columns once. Multi-week schedules populate automatically. Zero manual clicking.

Respect driver preferences and improve job acceptance rates

Configure shift preferences once. System auto-respects max hours, preferred days, and blocked availability. Higher job acceptance when drivers get preferred shifts.

Eliminate double-booking disasters by auto-blocking approved leave from allocation

Approved leave automatically blocks days in rota. System won't assign trips to unavailable drivers. No more scheduling conflicts or missed coverage.

How It Works

1

View the weekly grid

Open any week and see your full driver roster across 7 days. Each driver's availability and status displays in colour-coded cells.

2

Mark days and preferences

Click cells to set working days (WD), rest days (RD), holidays (HOL), or unavailable (OFF). Type shortcuts for rapid entry.

3

Bulk import or auto-allocate

Upload pre-planned rotas via CSV, or let the system suggest optimal allocations based on driver workload and preferences.

4

Review and publish

Check compliance, resolve conflicts, then lock the rota. Drivers see their schedule immediately on their devices.

Large transport operator (100+ drivers)

Drivers Who Don't Know Their Schedule Are Drivers Looking for Another Job

Rota scheduling is the foundation of predictable operations. Without it, you're juggling spreadsheets, missing compliance rules, causing driver burnout, and paying penalties. Drivers don't know when they're working. Compliance violations sneak through. Staff turnover climbs.

FleetYes's rota scheduling puts control in your hands and clarity in your drivers' hands. Visual grid scheduling cuts planning time from hours to minutes. Automatic compliance checking prevents violations before they happen. Drivers see their schedule instantly and stop calling to ask "Am I working Thursday?"

The result is immediate: 10x faster scheduling, zero compliance surprises, 30% fewer absences, and improved driver retention. You build rotas that work for the business and respect your team.
