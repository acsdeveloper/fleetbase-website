How It Works

1

Incur Expense

You purchase fuel, park your vehicle, pay a toll, or incur any business expense during your shift.

2

Capture Receipt

Open the expense section and photograph the receipt. The camera automatically detects the receipt in the frame and captures it with location metadata.

3

AI Extracts Details

Machine learning instantly extracts vendor name, amount, and date from the receipt photo. You review and confirm the data-or edit if needed.

4

Categorise & Submit

Select the expense category (the system may suggest one automatically). Add any notes, then submit for approval and reimbursement.

Receipts Pile Up. Reimbursements Don't. That's the Problem.

Expense reporting is where good intentions meet reality. You intend to submit expenses immediately. Reality: receipts pile up, you lose half of them, data entry takes forever, reimbursement takes weeks. Meanwhile, you're out-of-pocket for business expenses.

Our system kills every pain point. You photograph the receipt the moment you get it-no risk of losing it. The system extracts the data automatically-no manual typing. Everything's categorized instantly-no guessing or second-guessing. You submit, it goes through approval faster because the data is perfect, and your reimbursement arrives sooner.

For your company: They get immediate visibility into driver expenses by category and location instead of waiting for monthly reports. Expense processing is faster because the data arrives clean and categorized. And they have perfect records for audits, tax time, or investigations. Everyone wins when expense processing is automated.
