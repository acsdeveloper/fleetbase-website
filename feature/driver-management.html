Driver Management

Driver management gives you complete visibility into your workforce in a unified interface. The main view shows a driver roster listing all drivers with their status (active, on leave, unavailable, departed), searchable and filterable by team, location, or status. Click any driver to open their full profile card. The profile shows their complete history: contact info, assigned vehicles, qualifications, CPC certification status, and DVLA licence status (automatically checked against DVLA records, so you always know if their licence is valid). You see hours worked this week and this month, trips completed, incident history, and all training records in one place. An availability calendar shows each driver's approved leave, holidays, and unavailability blocks at a glance. Certification tracking is automatic: CPC training and licence expiry dates are tracked with countdown alerts appearing 60, 30, and 7 days before expiry. Any overdue renewals are flagged in red so nothing slips through. When a driver completes training, the completion is logged automatically (uploaded via mobile app or manually if needed). Performance metrics appear on the driver card: trip completion rate, on-time delivery rate, customer ratings, and safety incident history. This data-driven view supports fair performance assessments and coaching conversations. For bulk operations, a bulk import tool lets you upload a CSV or connect to your HR system. Map columns once (name, email, DOB, licence number, etc.), and the system imports 100+ drivers automatically with correct role assignment. On mobile, drivers see their rota, upcoming trips, training status, and certification expiry dates. They can request time off or propose shift swaps with a peer, all with one tap-improving transparency and reducing manual coordination.

Why Choose Driver Management

Driver onboarding and turnover cost thousands and slow operations. Without centralized management, you're juggling paper files, chasing certification renewals, missing training deadlines, and losing tribal knowledge when experienced drivers leave.

FleetYes's driver management centralizes everything and prevents compliance gaps. Bulk import drivers from your HR system in minutes. DVLA integration automatically validates licences. Training records are tracked with expiry alerts. Drivers have full visibility into their availability, assignments, and career within the company.

The result is immediate: bulk onboarding in hours instead of days, zero missed training deadlines, reduced turnover through visibility and fairness, and zero compliance surprises. Your team is always compliant and engaged.
