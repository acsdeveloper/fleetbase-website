How It Works

1

View Schedule

Open the schedule view to see your assigned shifts for the next week, month, or custom period. Each shift shows date, time, location, and assigned vehicle.

2

Request Time Off

Select a date on the calendar and tap "Request Leave." Choose your reason (vacation, sick leave, personal), add optional notes, and submit instantly.

3

Track Approval

Your request goes to management for approval. Check the status anytime-pending approval, approved, or needs more information.

4

Receive Reminders

Get notifications about your upcoming shifts. Know exactly when and where you need to be to start your delivery route.

Your Schedule Lives in Too Many Places and Nowhere at Once

Traditional schedule management is chaos. Drivers call their manager about time off. The manager checks the schedule (which is still in email or a spreadsheet), approves verbally, and then forgets to update anything. Days later, there's confusion about who's actually working. Someone thought they had Tuesday off and finds out they're expected in. Shift swaps happen verbally and never get recorded.

Our system creates a single source of truth. You see your schedule weeks in advance. You request time off and get instant approval. Everything is documented. Everything is clear.

For your company: Clear schedules mean better coverage planning. They can see leave requests instantly and plan accordingly. Shift swaps are documented for audit purposes. And drivers are happier because the anxiety and confusion about scheduling disappears.
