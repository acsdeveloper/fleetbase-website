How It Works

1

Enter Your Identifier

Open the login screen and enter your phone number or email address depending on your preference.

2

Receive Verification Code

A one-time code is sent to your phone (SMS) or email. This code proves you control that phone number or email address.

3

Confirm Code

Enter the verification code into the app. The system validates it and grants you access if the code is correct.

4

Secure Session

Once authenticated, your device is linked to your account. Future logins on the same device may be faster or use biometric authentication.

Biometric Secure Authentication

The login screen displays two input methods: phone number field and email field. Users select their preferred method and enter their identifier. They receive a code via SMS or email and return to the app. A code entry screen shows six digit input fields. Once entered correctly, they're logged in and see their dashboard. In settings, a "Manage Sessions" screen lists all active login sessions with device names, login dates, and locations.

Why Choose Biometric Secure Authentication

Passwords fail. People reuse passwords across services. People write them down. People forget them. And when passwords are compromised in data breaches, your account is at risk from strangers with stolen credentials. OTP authentication eliminates all of this.

You prove access to your phone number or email-something much harder to compromise than a reused password. No forgotten passwords. No password reset emails. No password manager juggling. Just verify possession of something you already have and you're in. It's more secure than passwords, more convenient than passwords, and simpler than passwords.

For your company: Better security without the usability headaches. Drivers don't struggle with complex password policies. Account access is completely logged and auditable. Lost devices don't expose your company because session management allows you to remotely terminate access. You get strong security and a better user experience-both, not either-or.
