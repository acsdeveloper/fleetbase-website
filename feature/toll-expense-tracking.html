Toll & Expense Tracking

Toll and expense tracking is transparent, automated, and built for fast reconciliation. The expense dashboard sits at the top with KPI cards showing tolls captured this month, parking costs, fines recorded, and total expenses recovered. Weekly and monthly trend charts show patterns (peak tolling hours, seasonal peaks) so you can forecast costs. Below that is an expense transaction log showing every toll, parking fee, and fine: date, associated trip, expense type, amount, location, and receipt status. Filter by trip, driver, location, or type to find specific expenses quickly. Expense capture happens directly on mobile. A driver photographs a receipt or payment confirmation, and the app captures it with a timestamp and geotag showing where the expense occurred. The receipt image is stored against the expense record. OCR technology automatically analyses the receipt image and extracts key data (date, amount, merchant, location), populating those fields for you to review and approve. The system automatically links the expense to the associated trip based on date and driver, but you can manually link it if needed. A trip detail view shows all associated tolls and parking costs, so customers get charged accurately. Expenses over a configured threshold require manager approval, where the manager reviews the receipt image and amount and can approve, reject, or request clarification. Approved expenses are automatically added to the customer invoice, with invoice totals including tolls, parking, and pass-through costs, then exported to your accounting system. A reconciliation report runs automatically, comparing expenses claimed against expenses captured, and highlights discrepancies (e.g., a toll billed by the toll authority but not yet captured in the system). This supports end-of-month reconciliation and audit work.
