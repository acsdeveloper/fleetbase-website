Vehicle Management

Vehicle management gives you complete fleet health visibility in one place. The main view shows a vehicle roster listing all your vehicles with their status (in use, available, maintenance, unavailable), filterable by vehicle type, location, or status. A month-view maintenance calendar sits prominently, showing upcoming and ongoing maintenance across your entire fleet. Items due soon appear in yellow, overdue items in red. Click any maintenance item to schedule service, log completion, or add notes. For each vehicle, a detailed card shows the complete history: registration, VIN, purchase date, mileage, MOT expiry, tachograph expiry, last service date, and next service due. A cost summary appears on the card for this month and year (maintenance, fuel, insurance), making it easy to identify high-cost vehicles. A compliance checklist shows green checkmarks for current MOT, tachograph, PMI, and insurance-and red flags for any overdue items with exact due dates on hover. Scheduled maintenance automatically appears as unavailability blocks on the calendar, and the allocation system automatically won't assign those vehicles during maintenance windows. You never accidentally dispatch a vehicle that's booked for service. A cost-per-vehicle dashboard shows spending across maintenance, fuel, and insurance so you can identify which vehicles are expensive to operate and may need replacement. On mobile, mechanics can update vehicle status from the workshop or roadside, log service completion, and record maintenance notes without paper forms or manual entry.

Why Choose Vehicle Management

Vehicle downtime costs thousands in lost revenue and operational disruption. Without proactive maintenance, breakdowns happen unexpectedly. Vehicles miss regulatory deadlines (MOT, tachograph, PMI). You scramble for replacement vehicles, cancel trips, disappoint customers.

FleetYes's vehicle management prevents breakdowns before they happen. Calendar views show upcoming maintenance deadlines. Vehicles are automatically marked unavailable during service windows, preventing accidental assignment. Historical records prove compliance to inspectors.

The result is immediate: 80% reduction in unplanned downtime, regulatory deadlines never missed, and complete maintenance audit trails. Your fleet stays healthy and revenue-generating.
