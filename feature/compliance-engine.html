Compliance Engine

The compliance engine works silently in the background, continuously monitoring every shift, every trip, and every driver against all applicable UK and EU regulations. The compliance dashboard gives you a weekly snapshot: violations detected this week at the top, followed by violations flagged for individual drivers, and compliance score trends over time. All shown in red/yellow/green status so you see health at a glance. Click any violation to open a detail panel showing exactly what rule was breached (e.g., "Driver exceeded 56-hour weekly limit by 2 hours") and which trips or shifts triggered it. See the driver's workload that caused the violation so you understand the root cause. When a dispatcher goes to assign a trip, the system runs a pre-journey validation check automatically: Is the driver within their weekly hours? Have they had sufficient rest since their last shift? Does their vehicle pass safety checks? The dispatcher sees either a green "Clear to dispatch" button or a red flag listing the specific violations preventing the assignment. No guessing, no manual checking. In the rota-scheduling interface, compliance violations appear instantly on the grid-red cells mark unsafe shifts so managers see problems before publishing the rota. Drivers can see their own hours remaining, rest requirements, and next allowed start time, so they understand why they can't work certain days. This transparency builds a culture where compliance is understood and respected. When violations are detected, the system escalates automatically: drivers approaching their rest limit get notified with how much time remains, and critical violations route immediately to the manager or safety officer. For regulatory submission, generate a compliance report showing all rules checked, violations found, corrective actions taken, and the evidence trail. Auditors see proof of diligence and governance.
