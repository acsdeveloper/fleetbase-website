Leave & Absence Management

Leave management puts transparency and control in managers' hands, with complete visibility into team coverage and leave balances. A month-view leave calendar shows all drivers and their approved leave, colour-coded by type (vacation in blue, sick in orange, unpaid in grey). Hover over any date to see the driver's name and leave type. You instantly see coverage per day without digging. A leave request queue shows all pending requests with driver name, requested dates, leave type, and reason. Approve or reject with one click, and add comments (e.g., "Approved – found cover with John"). A coverage forecast shows how many drivers are available each day: green means good coverage, yellow means tight, red means understaffed. Drill down to see which specific drivers are unavailable and why (on leave, sick, unavailable). For any driver, click to open their leave card showing leave history, current balance, and upcoming requests. Their leave entitlement (annual, sick, unpaid) and remaining balance appear clearly so they know how much leave they have left. To reduce formal leave requests, drivers can request shift swaps directly with peers—the app shows both drivers' availability, the manager reviews the swap and approves if coverage is maintained. Unexpected absences are logged in the system (call-outs, no-shows), and absence patterns are tracked to identify reliability issues and support performance conversations or disciplinary actions if needed. On mobile, drivers request leave, view approval status, and receive notifications of approval or denial. They can propose shift swaps with peers with one tap. This transparency improves driver satisfaction. Once leave is finalised, the system exports leave data to your payroll system, where hours marked as leave are automatically deducted from pay calculations—eliminating the manual HR-to-payroll handoff.

Why Choose Leave & Absence Management

Leave management chaos creates staffing gaps, confusion, and compliance violations. Without visibility, drivers request leave via phone or email, you manually track it on a spreadsheet, approvals get delayed, and trips get assigned to unavailable drivers. Drivers miss trips. Customers complain. Fines follow. Worse, drivers feel disrespected because their leave requests disappear into a black hole.

FleetYes's leave management puts transparency and control in one place. Drivers request leave through mobile app, manager sees impact on coverage instantly, approvals happen in seconds, and unavailable drivers are automatically excluded from scheduling. Drivers have peace of mind knowing their request is tracked. Managers have confidence that they won't over-allocate.

The result is immediate: coverage forecasting built in, payroll integration automated, and zero "surprise" staffing gaps. Fairness increases driver satisfaction, compliance violations disappear, and operations run smoothly.
