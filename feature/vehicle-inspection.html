How It Works

1

Driver checks vehicle before accepting trip

Opens mobile app. Walks around vehicle and takes photos. Notes any damage or mechanical issues.

2

System stores inspection data

Photos geotagged with location and timestamp. Damage severity flagged (minor, major, needs repair).

3

Manager reviews for issues

Dashboard shows flagged damage. Critical issues trigger alerts. Service team notified to address before next trip.

4

Post-trip inspection confirms no new damage

Driver repeats inspection after trip. Comparison shows what changed during delivery. Disputes resolved with photo evidence.

Why Choose Vehicle Inspection & Walkaround

Vehicle disputes cost money and damage customer relationships-and without proof, you lose. Customers claim vehicles arrived damaged. You claim they were fine when we left. No photos, no timestamps, no proof. Insurance denies claims. You eat the cost.

FleetYes's vehicle inspection creates timestamped, geotagged photographic proof. Before the trip starts, drivers walk around the vehicle, capture photos of condition, note any existing damage. After the trip, they inspect again. Photos are timestamped and geotagged. Disputes are resolved instantly with visual evidence.

The result is immediate: disputes resolved in seconds, 40% reduction in insurance claims, and complete pre/post-trip documentation. Customers see transparency and accountability. You see protection against false claims.
