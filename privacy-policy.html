Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20-01-2025

This Privacy Policy describes how FleetYes we collects, uses, and protects the personal information you provide on our website.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other details you provide when creating an account or contacting us.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, such as IP address, browser type, and browsing behavior.
  • Location Data: With your consent, we may collect GPS data or other location-based information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, manage, and improve our services.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Your Rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@fleetyes.com.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no online transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these sites.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated policy will be posted on this page with the "Effective Date" revised accordingly.
