# Operational Dashboard

## Key Benefits

- **Eliminate operational surprises by spotting problems in seconds on a single dashboard**
  Unassigned trips, drivers on leave, vehicles down, compliance violations—all visible instantly. One screen shows full operational status. No digging for info.

- **Speed decision-making by 50% through intelligent alerts that surface only critical issues**
  Smart alerts distinguish critical problems from routine information. No alert fatigue. Managers focus on what actually needs action. Decisions made faster.

- **Prevent missed information with real-time data that updates continuously without manual refresh**
  Trip status changes instantly. Driver availability updated live. Zero "why wasn't I told?" moments. Complete visibility at every moment.

- **Reduce context-switching and focus on relevant metrics through role-customised dashboards**
  Dispatcher sees unassigned trips and driver availability. Manager sees compliance and workload. Finance sees costs. Everyone sees exactly what they need.

- **Manage your entire network from one screen with aggregate data across all depots and franchises**
  See consolidated KPIs across 20+ depots. Drill down to individual location data in one click. Scale without losing visibility.

- **Forecast trends and optimise operations using historical data analysis and pattern recognition**
  Week-over-week trip volume analysis shows seasonal patterns. Cost trends identify efficiency improvements. Data-driven decisions improve margins continuously.

- **Achieve zero information loss during shift handovers with shared live dashboard**
  Night shift and day shift see identical real-time data. Information automatically passed. No gaps during transitions. Coverage seamless.

- **Track progress toward goals with customisable KPI targets and automatic variance alerts**
  Set target trip volume, utilisation rate, or compliance score. Dashboard shows progress visually. Alerts when targets missed. Accountability built in.

## How It Works

1. **Open the dashboard** — See real-time KPIs and alerts on load. No refresh needed.

2. **Review KPI cards** — Trips today, available drivers, fleet size, week's trips. Click to drill down.

3. **Check alerts** — Unassigned trips, drivers at rest limit, vehicles overdue for maintenance. Address top priority first.

4. **Customise for your role** — Add, remove, or reorder widgets. Save your preferred layout.

## Core Capabilities

- **Real-time KPI cards** — Trips today, active drivers, fleet utilisation, vehicle downtime, compliance status. Auto-refreshing data.

- **Contextual alert system** — Flags only issues requiring action. Distinguishes critical (block operation), warning (address soon), and info (FYI) alerts.

- **Custom dashboard widgets** — Add, remove, or rearrange widgets. Different roles see different dashboards by default.

- **Drill-down analytics** — Click KPI to see underlying data. "10 unassigned trips" → see which trips, which time windows, which locations.

- **Weekly/monthly trend charts** — Line charts show trip volume, cost, and compliance over time. Spot patterns and seasonality.

- **Driver workload summary** — See which drivers are heavily scheduled and who has capacity. Proactive workload balancing.

- **Vehicle status overview** — Vehicles in use, in maintenance, available, and unavailable. Quick view of fleet health.

- **Multi-location rollup** — Aggregate data across depots, franchises, or geographic regions. Drill into individual locations.

## Use Cases

- **Dispatch centre during busy hours** — Dispatcher sees unassigned trips count, available drivers grid, and vehicle status. Quickly allocates trips without hunting through data.

- **Fleet owner checking operations from home** — Opens app. Sees KPIs, any critical alerts, compliance status. Confidence everything is running normally.

- **Night shift handover** — Night manager and day manager review dashboard together. No information lost. Day manager knows what happened overnight.

- **Weekly operations review meeting** — Open dashboard, review weekly trends. Trip volume up 15%, compliance violations down. Data-driven discussion.

- **Franchise performance monitoring** — HQ reviews franchise-level KPIs. One franchise has high vehicle downtime. Triggers conversation with franchise owner.

- **Cost control focus** — CFO adds fuel cost and toll expense widgets. Weekly dashboard review shows cost per trip trending up. Investigation identifies root cause.

- **Safety and compliance audit** — Dashboard shows compliance violations by driver and by week. Data supports safety training prioritisation.

- **System health and monitoring** — Data team monitors data freshness, API health, and integration status. Alerts on connection failures or data sync delays.

## Visual Overview

The operational dashboard is your control centre. On load, you see a clean, intuitive layout built around your role. At the top sits a row of real-time KPI cards showing trips today (count and status breakdown), available drivers (count and utilisation %), fleet health (vehicles in use, available, maintenance), and compliance score. Below that is an alert zone, sorted by severity: critical issues in red (action required now), warnings in orange (address within hours), and info items in blue (FYI). Each alert links directly to the data you need to fix it, so you're never hunting through menus.

The main content area is a fully customisable widget grid. Dispatcher working a shift? Add "unassigned trips", "available drivers", and "vehicle status" widgets. Manager reviewing performance? Add "cost this week", "compliance violations", and "driver hours". Finance controller? Add "revenue recognised", "expenses pending approval", and "fuel cost trend". You drag widgets around, resize them, and save your layout. Week-over-week trip volume, cost trends, and compliance trends appear as simple charts that let you swipe between periods to spot patterns.

Every KPI and alert is clickable. Click any alert or KPI number, and you instantly see the underlying data—which trips are unassigned, which drivers are available, which vehicles are down for maintenance. No extra clicks, no "go to another screen and search"—you're there. On mobile, the same KPIs and alerts are optimized for small screens, and critical alerts push notifications to your phone so you're never out of the loop. The layout adapts to your role automatically; different users see different priorities and different dashboards.
