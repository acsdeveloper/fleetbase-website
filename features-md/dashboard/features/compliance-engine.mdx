## How It Works

1. **Assign a trip or schedule a shift** — You propose a driver for a trip or add them to a rota day.

2. **System checks 6 compliance rules in milliseconds** — Rest gaps, daily/weekly hours, biweekly limits, weekly rest periods, trip overlaps, and custom policies.

3. **Get instant feedback** — Green: safe to assign. Red: violation. Yellow: warning (approaching limit). See exactly which rule triggered the flag.

4. **Assign with confidence or adjust** — Proceed if safe, or change driver/trip/date to resolve the violation. All decisions logged for audit.

## Core Capabilities

- **6 built-in UK/EU regulations** — Enforces EC 561/2006 and WTD 2002/15/EC: minimum 11h rest, 9h max daily hours, 56h weekly limit, 90h biweekly limit, 46h weekly rest, and no trip overlaps.

- **Real-time pre-journey validation** — Check compliance before saving an assignment. Prevents violations before they happen. No post-incident discovery.

- **Prospective and retrospective checking** — Validate future schedules before dispatch. Also audit completed rotas against tachograph records to identify past violations.

- **Custom policy rules** — Override default EU limits with your company's stricter policies (e.g., 10h daily max instead of 9h). Different rules per driver or team.

- **Driver fatigue alerts** — Highlight drivers approaching rest limits or excessive hours. Proactively suggest rest before violations occur.

- **Violation severity levels** — Critical (blocks assignment), Warning (inform before proceeding), Info (notify without blocking). Configure per rule.

- **Bulk compliance audit** — Upload rota and tachograph data. System identifies all violations across the fleet. Export audit report with recommended corrections.

- **Integration with shift allocation** — Auto-allocation engine respects all compliance rules. No need to manually check after AI suggests assignments.

## Visual Overview

The compliance engine works silently in the background, continuously monitoring every shift, every trip, and every driver against all applicable UK and EU regulations. The compliance dashboard gives you a weekly snapshot: violations detected this week at the top, followed by violations flagged for individual drivers, and compliance score trends over time. All shown in red/yellow/green status so you see health at a glance. Click any violation to open a detail panel showing exactly what rule was breached (e.g., "Driver exceeded 56-hour weekly limit by 2 hours") and which trips or shifts triggered it. See the driver's workload that caused the violation so you understand the root cause.

When a dispatcher goes to assign a trip, the system runs a pre-journey validation check automatically: Is the driver within their weekly hours? Have they had sufficient rest since their last shift? Does their vehicle pass safety checks? The dispatcher sees either a green "Clear to dispatch" button or a red flag listing the specific violations preventing the assignment. No guessing, no manual checking. In the rota-scheduling interface, compliance violations appear instantly on the grid—red cells mark unsafe shifts so managers see problems before publishing the rota. Drivers can see their own hours remaining, rest requirements, and next allowed start time, so they understand why they can't work certain days. This transparency builds a culture where compliance is understood and respected.

When violations are detected, the system escalates automatically: drivers approaching their rest limit get notified with how much time remains, and critical violations route immediately to the manager or safety officer. For regulatory submission, generate a compliance report showing all rules checked, violations found, corrective actions taken, and the evidence trail. Auditors see proof of diligence and governance.
