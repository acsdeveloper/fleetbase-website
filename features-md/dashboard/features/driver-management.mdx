# Driver Management

## Key Benefits

- **Reduce new driver onboarding from days to hours through bulk import and automated assignment**
  Upload 100 drivers from Excel. System auto-assigns to teams, depots, and shifts. All compliance validation automatic. New team ready to work same day.

- **Eliminate the risk of operating with invalid drivers through real-time DVLA licence verification**
  System flags expired, suspended, or invalid licences immediately. Prevents operating with non-compliant drivers. Regulatory risk eliminated.

- **Prevent over-scheduling and driver fatigue through real-time availability visibility**
  Dashboard shows who's on leave, approaching fatigue limits, or already fully booked. Allocate trips only to available, fresh drivers. Safety improves.

- **Cut admin overhead and confusion with transparent, logged driver communications**
  Drivers see their schedule, assigned trips, and compliance status in app. No confusion about shifts or requirements. Support tickets drop 40%.

- **Stay CPC-compliant effortlessly with automatic training record tracking and renewal alerts**
  Upload training certificates. System reads expiry dates. Alerts driver and manager 90 days before renewal. Zero lapsed certifications. DVSA ready.

- **Reduce turnover by 20% through fair workload distribution and preventing driver burnout**
  Workload analytics show who's overloaded. Rebalance proactively. Fair treatment improves retention. Recruitment costs drop significantly.

- **Scale capacity instantly by activating pre-vetted contractor pool during peak periods**
  Maintain pre-vetted contractor list. Activate instantly when needed. No weeks spent recruiting or onboarding. Surge capacity ready to deploy.

- **Prevent accidents and improve safety culture through incident tracking and coaching**
  Log accidents, near-misses, and safety concerns. Link to training and performance history. Data-driven safety improvements reduce incident rates.

## How It Works

1. **Import driver data** — Upload driver list (name, contact, licence, DBS status). System validates licence against DVLA.

2. **Assign to teams and preferences** — Link drivers to depots or teams. Set shift preferences, max hours, and blocked availability.

3. **Schedule and monitor** — Build rotas and assign trips. Real-time dashboard shows driver status, availability, and compliance.

4. **Track training and incidents** — Log completed CPC training, near-misses, and disciplinary actions. System alerts before compliance lapses.

## Core Capabilities

- **Driver registry and contact management** — Centralised driver database with contact info, licence details, DBS status, emergency contacts, and job history.

- **Bulk driver import** — Upload driver roster from Excel. Automatic field mapping and validation. Batch-update driver details without manual entry.

- **DVLA licence integration** — Real-time licence checks. Flags suspended, expired, or invalid licences before driver is allocated trips.

- **Driver availability and preferences** — Set preferred shift times, maximum weekly hours, maximum consecutive days, and blocked dates (education, personal, etc.).

- **Driver CPC compliance tracking** — Log completed training modules and dates. System alerts before 35-hour annual refresher is overdue. Export training audit.

- **Incident and behaviour logging** — Record accidents, near-misses, speeding, harsh braking. Link to training recommendations. Build individual safety profile.

- **Workload analytics** — Monitor hours worked, trips completed, and revenue generated per driver. Identify over-scheduled and under-utilised drivers.

- **Driver app and communication** — Drivers receive rotas, assigned trips, and messages via mobile app. Accept/decline shifts, upload walkaround photos, mark trip complete.

## Visual Overview

Driver management gives you complete visibility into your workforce in a unified interface. The main view shows a driver roster listing all drivers with their status (active, on leave, unavailable, departed), searchable and filterable by team, location, or status. Click any driver to open their full profile card. The profile shows their complete history: contact info, assigned vehicles, qualifications, CPC certification status, and DVLA licence status (automatically checked against DVLA records, so you always know if their licence is valid). You see hours worked this week and this month, trips completed, incident history, and all training records in one place.

An availability calendar shows each driver's approved leave, holidays, and unavailability blocks at a glance. Certification tracking is automatic: CPC training and licence expiry dates are tracked with countdown alerts appearing 60, 30, and 7 days before expiry. Any overdue renewals are flagged in red so nothing slips through. When a driver completes training, the completion is logged automatically (uploaded via mobile app or manually if needed). Performance metrics appear on the driver card: trip completion rate, on-time delivery rate, customer ratings, and safety incident history. This data-driven view supports fair performance assessments and coaching conversations.

For bulk operations, a bulk import tool lets you upload a CSV or connect to your HR system. Map columns once (name, email, DOB, licence number, etc.), and the system imports 100+ drivers automatically with correct role assignment. On mobile, drivers see their rota, upcoming trips, training status, and certification expiry dates. They can request time off or propose shift swaps with a peer, all with one tap—improving transparency and reducing manual coordination.
