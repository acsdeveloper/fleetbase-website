# Staff Training & Compliance Documents

## Key Benefits

- **Eliminate CPC renewal surprises with automatic expiry tracking and 90-day advance alerts**
  System tracks every CPC completion date. Alerts driver and manager 90 days before expiry. Zero missed deadlines. 100% driver retention of valid certification.

- **Pass DVSA inspections in minutes by exporting complete, audit-ready training documentation**
  DVSA requests training records? One-click export shows every driver's completion dates, instructors, topics, and validation evidence. Inspection passed immediately.

- **Eliminate compliance risk by maintaining single-source-of-truth driver qualification database**
  Stop hunting through files and emails for CPC proof. Centralised system shows every driver's valid certifications. One dashboard. Complete visibility.

- **Cut training admin overhead by 70% through bulk certificate upload and automatic date parsing**
  Batch upload 50 CPC certificates. System reads expiry dates automatically. Stores in driver record. Zero manual date entry errors. Admin freed up instantly.

- **Empower drivers with transparent access to their own training status and renewal requirements**
  Drivers see CPC expiry date and renewal deadline in their app. Submit completed training for manager approval. Self-service reduces admin burden.

- **Speed audit completion from hours to minutes with automated compliance report generation**
  Pull monthly report showing every driver's valid/expired CPC, DBS, medical status in one click. Auditable timestamp on every record. Finance and compliance ready.

- **Build driver development culture by tracking mandatory and optional professional training**
  Track CPC, DBS, customer training, safety courses, advanced certifications. Driver development creates culture of excellence and improves retention.

- **Reduce paperwork friction with pre-built compliance templates and instant document access**
  Drivers download CPC or training templates from app. Manager approves submissions instantly. Zero back-and-forth emails. Faster compliance cycles.

## How It Works

1. **Upload training records** — Driver completes CPC course. Uploads certificate via app or manager uploads batch.

2. **System validates and stores** — System reads certificate date and stores in driver record. Auto-sets renewal due date (35h every 5 years for CPC).

3. **Track compliance** — Dashboard shows which drivers have valid training. Alerts when expiry approaches.

4. **Export for audit** — Regulator inspection? Export training audit report with completion dates and evidence.

## Visual Overview

Training and compliance document tracking is your regulatory safeguard, keeping all certifications and training records in one auditable place. A compliance calendar displays a month view of all training expiry dates across your team, colour-coded by type (CPC, DBS, medical exam, etc.) and status (current, expiring soon, overdue). Hover over any date to see the driver's name and exact expiry date. For any driver, click to open their training card showing all certifications: CPC training dates (initial and refresher), completion dates, expiry dates, medical exam status, DBS check status and expiry, and more. You can upload new certificates directly to the card.

A document library sits as your centralised repository of all training records and compliance documents. Upload certificates, training completion records, and company policies, and search by driver name, document type, or date. An expiry alerts dashboard prominently displays training expiring in 30 days, expiring in 7 days, and already overdue, sorted by urgency. Click any alert to schedule refresher training or send a notification asking the driver to submit their renewal certificate.

The scheduling system automatically integrates with training status: drivers with overdue training are automatically excluded from shift allocation, and the system won't schedule them for trips. This prevents regulatory violations before they happen. Automatic email reminders are sent to drivers and managers 60 days before expiry, 30 days before, 7 days before, and on the expiry date. Push notifications on the driver app keep them informed. When it's time for a regulatory inspection, export a compliance report in DVSA-ready format showing all drivers, training dates, expiry dates, completion records, with certificates attached and full audit trail included. On mobile, drivers can photograph their completion certificate or exam result and upload it directly to their training record; the manager receives a notification to approve the upload.
