## How It Works

1. **View the weekly grid** — Open any week and see your full driver roster across 7 days. Each driver's availability and status displays in colour-coded cells.

2. **Mark days and preferences** — Click cells to set working days (WD), rest days (RD), holidays (HOL), or unavailable (OFF). Type shortcuts for rapid entry.

3. **Bulk import or auto-allocate** — Upload pre-planned rotas via CSV, or let the system suggest optimal allocations based on driver workload and preferences.

4. **Review and publish** — Check compliance, resolve conflicts, then lock the rota. Drivers see their schedule immediately on their devices.

## Visual Overview

The rota interface is your weekly operational blueprint, laid out as a clean 7-day grid where rows represent your drivers and columns represent each day of the week. Each cell shows the driver's status for that day (WD for working day, RD for rest day, OFF for unavailable, HOL for holiday), colour-coded so you see compliance at a glance. On the left runs a driver sidebar listing all your drivers with a workload summary for each: hours scheduled this week, trips assigned, and leave approved. Sort the list by workload, availability, or location to find the right person fast.

At the top of the grid is a compliance indicator showing red/yellow/green status for your entire rota. Hover over it to see exactly which violations are flagged (excessive hours, insufficient rest, unsafe shift patterns). A bulk edit toolbar sits above the grid with quick actions: mark multiple drivers as working, apply a template rota to the whole week, swap driver pairs in one click, or approve all pending changes at once. Approved leave, holidays, and blocked dates overlay on the grid in a separate layer, making it visually clear which days drivers are unavailable. The system prevents accidental assignment to those days.

On the mobile app, drivers see their week displayed simply: their working days, rest days, upcoming shifts, and rest requirements. They can request time off or swap shifts with a peer with just one tap. In the admin view, an audit trail panel shows the full change history: who modified the rota, when, and exactly what changed. Export the audit trail for regulatory proof during inspections.
