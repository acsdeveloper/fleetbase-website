# Vehicle Management

## Key Benefits

- **Eliminate surprise vehicle failures and MOT penalties with automatic compliance tracking**
  System alerts 30 days before MOT, tachograph calibration, and PMI checks are due. Zero missed deadlines. Zero regulatory fines.

- **Reduce unplanned downtime by 80% and avoid revenue loss through proactive maintenance scheduling**
  Schedule maintenance before failures occur. Know next service due date in advance. Plan around maintenance instead of reacting to breakdowns.

- **Prevent costly downtime by automatically excluding vehicles under maintenance from trip allocation**
  When vehicle goes into service, system won't assign new trips. No wasted fuel or missed deliveries. Coverage maintained automatically.

- **Cut fleet operating costs by 15-20% through cost-per-mile analysis and vehicle optimisation**
  See which vehicles generate profit and which drain margins. Make data-driven decisions about fleet size, vehicle type, and retirements.

- **Onboard 500+ vehicle fleet in under an hour with bulk import and automatic validation**
  Upload vehicle data. System validates plate, VIN, and service history. Flags missing critical information. Zero manual data entry errors.

- **Eliminate compliance portal switching and manual status checking with real-time DVLA integration**
  MOT status pulled live from DVLA. Tachograph calibration tracked automatically. All compliance data in one dashboard. Zero separate logins needed.

- **Pass insurance audits and improve resale valuation with complete maintenance proof**
  Every maintenance record, every driver assignment, every trip logged and timestamped. Prove due diligence to insurers. Document condition for resale.

- **Reduce driver frustration and support tickets by putting all vehicle info in driver's app**
  Drivers see fuel type, mechanical notes, known issues, and maintenance schedule. No hunting for keys or asking mechanics. Efficiency improves instantly.

## How It Works

1. **Add or import vehicles** — Enter manually or bulk-load from CSV. Capture plate, VIN, make, model, year, fuel type, and service history.

2. **Log maintenance and compliance** — Record MOT dates, tachograph calibration, PMI checks, and routine services. System tracks next due dates.

3. **View maintenance calendar** — See upcoming and overdue maintenance at a glance. Alerts notify you 30 days before critical dates.

4. **Allocate confidently** — System won't schedule trips to vehicles marked for maintenance. Automatically reassign to available vehicles.

## Core Capabilities

- **Centralised vehicle registry** — All vehicle data in one place: registration, VIN, fuel type, insurance expiry, maintenance history, driver assignments.

- **Automated MOT and regulatory tracking** — Integrates with DVLA data. Real-time status of MOT validity. No manual checking required.

- **Maintenance schedule management** — Log last service and next due date. Track costs and parts replaced. Calendar view shows all upcoming maintenance across fleet.

- **Vehicle downtime marking** — Mark a vehicle as unavailable (maintenance, repair, inspection). System automatically excludes it from trip allocation.

- **Cost tracking per vehicle** — Log fuel costs, maintenance, insurance, tax. Calculate cost-per-mile and cost-per-trip. Identify high-cost vehicles.

- **Driver vehicle assignment** — Link drivers to preferred vehicles. Supports multi-assignment (drivers can drive multiple vehicles). Reduce driver onboarding time.

- **Tachograph calibration tracking** — Calendar alerts for annual tachograph calibration due dates. Prevents non-compliant operation and fines.

- **Vehicle incident logging** — Record damage, accidents, or mechanical issues. Link to incident report. Track repair progress and costs.

## Use Cases

- **Medium-sized transport company (50+ vehicles)** — Track MOT, maintenance, and driver assignments across entire fleet. Automated alerts prevent expired certifications.

- **Parcel delivery network** — Mark vehicles as unavailable when they enter maintenance rotation. Auto-reassign pending trips to available vehicles. Continuous service.

- **Franchise operation** — Each franchise manages its own vehicle fleet. HQ sees consolidated compliance status. Identify fleet-wide trends and cost issues.

- **Vehicle leasing company** — Track lease expiry dates and maintenance obligations. Prove to lessor that vehicles were properly maintained. Support lease renewal or purchase decisions.

- **Construction and plant hire** — Track specialised equipment fitted to vehicles. Know which vehicles have which attachments. Prevent allocation of wrong vehicle to job.

- **Emergency response fleet** — Track vehicle readiness and maintenance status. Ensure backup vehicles are available. Reduce response delays due to vehicle unavailability.

- **Sustainability reporting** — Track fuel consumption per vehicle. Identify high-consumption vehicles. Make data-driven decisions about electric or efficient replacements.

- **Insurance and claims management** — Complete maintenance history proves due diligence in vehicle care. Reduces insurance premiums and claim denial risk.

## Integration & Compatibility

- **DVLA vehicle data** — Real-time MOT status and vehicle registration validation. No manual status checking.

- **Geotab telematics** — Connect vehicle performance data (fuel consumption, harsh braking, idle time) to maintenance records. Link driver behaviour to vehicle wear.

- **Insurance systems** — Export vehicle data with maintenance history to insurers. Prove compliance and proper care. Support premium negotiations.

- **Fuel receipts and expenses** — Link fuel purchases to vehicles. Calculate fuel economy and identify anomalies (theft, poor maintenance).

- **Trip allocation engine** — Vehicle availability status automatically fed to allocation system. Won't schedule unavailable vehicles.

- **Driver management** — Link drivers to vehicles for training and familiarisation. Track which drivers use which vehicles. Identify need for additional training.

- **Maintenance service providers** — Integrate with preferred maintenance partners. Automatic scheduling and service order generation based on due dates.

- **Accounting and cost centres** — Export vehicle costs (maintenance, fuel, insurance) by vehicle or cost centre. Support budget tracking and vehicle ROI analysis.

## Visual Overview

Vehicle management gives you complete fleet health visibility in one place. The main view shows a vehicle roster listing all your vehicles with their status (in use, available, maintenance, unavailable), filterable by vehicle type, location, or status. A month-view maintenance calendar sits prominently, showing upcoming and ongoing maintenance across your entire fleet. Items due soon appear in yellow, overdue items in red. Click any maintenance item to schedule service, log completion, or add notes. 

For each vehicle, a detailed card shows the complete history: registration, VIN, purchase date, mileage, MOT expiry, tachograph expiry, last service date, and next service due. A cost summary appears on the card for this month and year (maintenance, fuel, insurance), making it easy to identify high-cost vehicles. A compliance checklist shows green checkmarks for current MOT, tachograph, PMI, and insurance—and red flags for any overdue items with exact due dates on hover.

Scheduled maintenance automatically appears as unavailability blocks on the calendar, and the allocation system automatically won't assign those vehicles during maintenance windows. You never accidentally dispatch a vehicle that's booked for service. A cost-per-vehicle dashboard shows spending across maintenance, fuel, and insurance so you can identify which vehicles are expensive to operate and may need replacement. On mobile, mechanics can update vehicle status from the workshop or roadside, log service completion, and record maintenance notes without paper forms or manual entry.
