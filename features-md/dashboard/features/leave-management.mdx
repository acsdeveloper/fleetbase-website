# Leave & Absence Management

## Key Benefits

- **Eliminate double-booking disasters by automatically blocking drivers on approved leave from scheduling**
  Approved leave? Automatically marked unavailable in rota. System won't allocate trips. Zero scheduling conflicts. Zero wasted coverage.

- **Prevent last-minute staffing crises with transparent leave visibility across entire team**
  Everyone sees who's on leave and when. Team members can proactively request cover. Managers plan workload weeks in advance. No surprises.

- **Speed leave approval from email chains to instant decisions with streamlined workflow**
  Request submitted → manager notified automatically → approved/rejected in system → driver sees status immediately. No email lost in chaos.

- **Cut payroll processing time by 50% with automatic holiday entitlement calculation and tracking**
  System tracks accrued hours, used days, and remaining balance automatically. Supports full-time, part-time, and contract staff with different rules. Payroll ready.

- **Plan coverage confidently by forecasting absence impact on daily operations**
  Manager sees exactly how much coverage will be lost if leave is approved. Can make approve/reschedule decision with complete visibility. Better planning.

- **Enable flexible scheduling with peer-to-peer shift swaps that managers approve in one click**
  Driver requests shift swap with peer. Peer accepts. Manager approves if compliance and coverage maintained. Flexibility without chaos.

- **Stay compliant with working time regulations through integrated rest-day tracking**
  Leave integrated into compliance system. Ensures drivers get required rest and don't exceed consecutive working limits. Regulatory compliance automatic.

- **Resolve payroll and HR disputes instantly with timestamped, immutable leave records**
  Every leave request, approval, amendment logged with timestamp and user ID. Driver disputes about hours paid? Show proof. HR disputes settled instantly.

## How It Works

1. **Request leave** — Driver submits leave request via app. Specifies dates, type (holiday, sick, personal), and optional reason.

2. **Manager review** — Manager sees request impact on scheduling. Coverage forecast shows whether team can cover absence. Approves or reschedules.

3. **Automatic blocking** — Approved leave automatically marks driver as unavailable in rota. Won't be allocated to trips.

4. **Payroll integration** — Leave data exported to payroll system. Automatically reduces paid hours or deducts from holiday entitlement.

- **Leave request submission** — Drivers request leave via app or web portal. Specify dates, leave type, and optional reason.

- **Entitlement tracking** — System tracks accrued hours, used days, and remaining balance per driver. Enforces maximum usage limits.

## Visual Overview

Leave management puts transparency and control in managers' hands, with complete visibility into team coverage and leave balances. A month-view leave calendar shows all drivers and their approved leave, colour-coded by type (vacation in blue, sick in orange, unpaid in grey). Hover over any date to see the driver's name and leave type. You instantly see coverage per day without digging. A leave request queue shows all pending requests with driver name, requested dates, leave type, and reason. Approve or reject with one click, and add comments (e.g., "Approved – found cover with John"). 

A coverage forecast shows how many drivers are available each day: green means good coverage, yellow means tight, red means understaffed. Drill down to see which specific drivers are unavailable and why (on leave, sick, unavailable). For any driver, click to open their leave card showing leave history, current balance, and upcoming requests. Their leave entitlement (annual, sick, unpaid) and remaining balance appear clearly so they know how much leave they have left. To reduce formal leave requests, drivers can request shift swaps directly with peers—the app shows both drivers' availability, the manager reviews the swap and approves if coverage is maintained.

Unexpected absences are logged in the system (call-outs, no-shows), and absence patterns are tracked to identify reliability issues and support performance conversations or disciplinary actions if needed. On mobile, drivers request leave, view approval status, and receive notifications of approval or denial. They can propose shift swaps with peers with one tap. This transparency improves driver satisfaction. Once leave is finalised, the system exports leave data to your payroll system, where hours marked as leave are automatically deducted from pay calculations—eliminating the manual HR-to-payroll handoff.
