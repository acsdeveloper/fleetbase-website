# PROJECT OVERVIEW

FleetYes Navigator is a unified mobile operations platform for delivery drivers managing orders, routes, and logistics. It consolidates everything a driver needs—orders, navigation, expenses, compliance, and team communication—in a single app, eliminating the need for multiple tools and reducing friction in last-mile delivery operations.

---

# KEY BENEFITS

## Order & Delivery Management

- **Accept or reject jobs instantly from your phone**
  Real-time order assignments mean you're never kept waiting. View full delivery details—address, contact, payment, special instructions—before deciding to accept.

- **Track order status from assignment to completion**
  See exactly where each order stands: pending, in-progress, completed. Know instantly when new work arrives or when a job is cancelled.

- **Work seamlessly, even with poor connectivity**
  The app queues your actions offline and syncs automatically when you reconnect. No lost work, no frustration in dead zones.

## Navigation & Route Efficiency

- **Navigate using your preferred maps app**
  Launch directly to Google Maps, Apple Maps, or Waze. Real-time GPS tracking means operations teams can see your location and route progress.

- **See your full delivery route at a glance**
  View all waypoints, turn-by-turn directions, and optimised delivery sequences. Plan your day more efficiently.

## Proof of Delivery

- **Capture and verify proof of delivery instantly**
  Take photos directly in the app with automatic location metadata extraction (EXIF). No separate camera app needed.

## Expense & Receipt Management

- **Log expenses and capture receipts in seconds**
  AI-powered receipt recognition automatically extracts vendor, amount, and date from photos. No manual typing, no form-filling.

- **Track expenses and approval status**
  View all submitted expenses, categorised expenses (fuel, parking, tolls), and monitor which ones are approved or pending.

## Communication & Collaboration

- **Message your operations team and colleagues in real-time**
  Dedicated chat channels keep conversations organised by route, team, or urgency. See who's online and available.

- **Stay connected with persistent message history**
  All conversations are saved and searchable, so you can refer back to earlier instructions or context.

## Compliance & Safety

- **Complete mandatory safety checks before each shift**
  Vehicle walk-around checklists and pre-trip inspections ensure you and your vehicle are ready. Compliance tracking is automatic.

- **Access training and stay certified**
  Complete mandatory and elective training modules directly in the app. Track progress and certificate status in one place.

- **Manage your driver documents and certifications**
  Store licences, permits, insurance documents, and certifications in one secure location. Always have them accessible.

## Schedule & Leave Management

- **View your shifts and request time off easily**
  See your schedule at a glance. Submit leave requests with calendar integration and track approval status in real-time.

## Earnings & Payment Tracking

- **Monitor your daily earnings and payment history**
  View breakdown by date, track bonuses and deductions, and see settlement status. Multi-currency support shows earnings in your local rate.

## Settings & Customisation

- **Choose your language and theme**
  Multi-language support and dark mode adapt the app to your preference. Settings persist across sessions.

- **Configure the app for your organisation**
  Dynamic configuration via deep links lets the same app work with different back-end systems and organisations.

# THIRD-PARTY INTEGRATIONS

- **FleetYes SDK** — Order and driver data management
- **Google Maps / Apple Maps** — Navigation and routing
- **Waze** — Alternative navigation launch
- **ML Kit Text Recognition** — Automatic receipt OCR and data extraction
- **SocketCluster** — Real-time order and message updates via WebSocket
- **Push Notifications (iOS & Android)** — Instant alerts for new jobs and cancellations
- **React Native Vision Camera (iOS)** — High-quality photo capture for proof of delivery
- **React Native Geolocation Service** — Background GPS tracking during deliveries
- **Gifted Chat** — Team messaging and communication interface

# STANDOUT FEATURES

1. **Intelligent Expense Automation** — AI-powered receipt detection eliminates manual data entry. Take a photo, the app extracts vendor, amount, and date automatically.

2. **Works Without Internet** — Full offline support means drivers stay productive in areas with poor connectivity. Orders, updates, and submissions sync automatically when connection returns.

3. **Real-Time Operations Sync** — Live order updates and instant messaging via WebSocket keep the team coordinated. No delays, no missed notifications.

# GAPS & SUGGESTIONS

- **Digital Signatures** — Add signature capture for complex deliveries requiring customer sign-off.
- **Offline Maps** — Pre-download maps for areas with no connectivity instead of relying on real-time map data.
- **Biometric Login** — Face or fingerprint unlock for faster re-authentication on return shifts.
- **Voice Logging** — Hands-free expense and issue reporting via voice-to-text while driving.
- **Customer Ratings** — In-app rating system after delivery completion for feedback loop.
- **Driver Performance Dashboard** — Analytics showing on-time percentage, customer ratings, and efficiency metrics.
- **Receipt Confidence Scores** — Show how confident the AI is in extracted expense data for verification.
