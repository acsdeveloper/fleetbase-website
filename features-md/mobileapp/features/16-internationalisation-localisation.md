# Internationalisation & Localisation
## Your language. Your currency. Your format. Regional adaptation from day one

Building delivery operations across borders means managing language and currency complexity. Drivers in different countries speak different languages. Earnings display in different currencies. Date formats vary. Trying to run a global operation with an English-only, USD-only app creates confusion, mistakes, and support burden.

Our localisation system adapts to your driver's region automatically. Everything—language, dates, numbers, currency—displays according to local conventions. No confusion. No mistakes. No friction for international teams.

---

## Key Benefits

- **All Screens in Your Language**: Every menu, message, notification, and instruction appears in your language—no untranslated buttons or confusing English text
- **Seamless Regional Format**: Dates display in your regional format (DD/MM or MM/DD), numbers show with correct separators (1,000 vs 1.000), no ambiguity or errors
- **Automatic Currency Handling**: Earnings and amounts display in your local currency with live exchange rates—you always know what you're earning in your own money
- **Real-Time Conversion Across Borders**: Working across multiple countries? Currencies convert instantly and accurately for international reimbursement and earnings tracking
- **Your Preference, Always**: Language and region choices persist across every device you use—one setting, consistent experience everywhere
- **International Team Harmony**: Teams spanning multiple countries each get their native language and conventions—no language barrier, no format confusion, reduced support tickets
- **Works Offline**: Language and localisation settings work without internet—change language once, then offline mode still respects your choice
- **Accessible to Non-English Speakers**: Non-English speakers can confidently use the app in their native language—no language anxiety, better training outcomes

---

## How It Works

**1. Select Your Language**
During login or in settings, choose your preferred language. The app switches to that language immediately.

**2. Regional Settings Sync**
Your region settings automatically customise number formats, date formats, and currency display based on your language.

**3. Work in Your Language**
Every screen, menu, message, and notification appears in your language. You never encounter untranslated text.

**4. Preferences Persist**
Your language choice is saved to your account and applies across all devices you use.

## Why Choose This Feature

Apps in the wrong language create errors. Drivers misunderstand instructions. Data entry mistakes happen because the label was confusing. Important messages get missed because the icon was unclear. An app in a driver's native language eliminates all of these friction points.

For international companies, language support in the foundation means you can hire and train drivers globally without forcing them to operate in English. A company with teams in Berlin, Barcelona, and Toronto gives each team their native interface—better understanding, fewer errors, faster onboarding.

Currency handling is equally essential. When drivers work across borders, they need to know what they're earning in their own currency. Manual currency math is error-prone. Real-time conversion is seamless and accurate—drivers always know their earnings in their native currency, support tickets decrease, financial confidence increases.
