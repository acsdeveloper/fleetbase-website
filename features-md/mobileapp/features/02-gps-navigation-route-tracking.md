## How It Works

**1. View Your Route**
Open the order to see your complete delivery route with all stops plotted on an interactive map. Multiple waypoints clearly indicate each delivery location.

**2. Choose Your Navigator**
Select your preferred navigation app—Google Maps, Apple Maps, or Waze—with a single tap. Each offers different route optimisation algorithms.

**3. Navigate in Real-Time**
Follow turn-by-turn directions with live GPS tracking. Real-time position updates keep operations teams informed of your progress.

**4. Confirm Arrival**
Automatic arrival detection at delivery locations, or manually confirm completion to trigger proof-of-delivery capture.

## Why Choose This Feature

Navigation is where efficient delivery operations live or die. Good routing means more deliveries per day. Bad routing means wasted fuel, late arrivals, and frustrated customers. Most drivers still rely on phone GPS alone—treating each delivery as a separate journey instead of an optimised sequence.

Our system shows you the entire multi-stop route upfront, automatically sequenced for maximum efficiency. You're not reacting to navigation one delivery at a time; you're executing a choreographed route. Real-time traffic integration means you adapt to congestion automatically instead of discovering it when you turn the corner. And because you can choose Google Maps, Apple Maps, or Waze, you always use the routing intelligence that works best for your local area.

For your operations team, live GPS tracking means they know exactly where deliveries are without radio chatter. Customers get automated updates about arrival times. And you get the data to show you've genuinely completed every stop—no disputes, no ambiguity.
