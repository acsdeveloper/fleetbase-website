# Biometric & Secure Authentication
## Secure your account in seconds—no passwords to remember, no complex rules to follow

Passwords are the weak link in security. You reuse them. You write them down. You forget them. Someone finds them and your account is compromised. But traditional apps demand passwords because... well, that's what they've always done.

Our authentication system is modern. You log in with your phone number or email. A verification code arrives. You confirm. Done. No password to remember. No complexity to manage. No password reset emails. Security is stronger than passwords, convenience is dramatically better.

---

## Key Benefits

- **Password-Free Security That's Actually Stronger**: OTP authentication is more secure than passwords without the hassle of remembering complex strings—you use something you already have, your phone number or email
- **Instant Access Without Forgotten Passwords**: No "I forgot my password" panic, no 24-hour password reset waits, no account lockouts—you verify possession of your phone or email instantly
- **Works on Multiple Devices Safely**: Use the app on your phone, tablet, or even a shared device—each session is secure and individually manageable without password sharing risks
- **Instant Account Recovery if You Lose Access**: Lost your phone or changed your number? SMS or email verification gets you back in immediately instead of waiting for support tickets
- **No More Password Manager Complexity**: You're not managing another password in another app, you're not writing passwords down, you're not trying to remember "which variation did I use this time"
- **Device Verification Protects Against Loss**: If you lose your phone, you can log out all sessions from another device—account protection doesn't require password recovery
- **Account is Yours, Not Hackable**: OTP codes work only once and expire quickly—even if someone intercepts one, it's useless minutes later; breached password databases don't matter because you have no password
- **Session Control Gives You Complete Safety**: See all active sessions, see when and where you logged in, remotely terminate sessions if you spot suspicious activity—complete account control

## How It Works

**1. Enter Your Identifier**
Open the login screen and enter your phone number or email address depending on your preference.

**2. Receive Verification Code**
A one-time code is sent to your phone (SMS) or email. This code proves you control that phone number or email address.

**3. Confirm Code**
Enter the verification code into the app. The system validates it and grants you access if the code is correct.

**4. Secure Session**
Once authenticated, your device is linked to your account. Future logins on the same device may be faster or use biometric authentication.
