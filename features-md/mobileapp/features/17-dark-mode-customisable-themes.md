# Dark Mode & Customisable Themes
## Dark mode by default, light mode when you need it, brand colours throughout—visual comfort meets identity

Late-night deliveries with a bright white screen drains your eyes, drains your battery on AMOLED phones, and strains your vision. Yet most apps force you to choose: light mode only, or deal with black text and white backgrounds.

Our theme system adapts to you. Dark mode protects your eyes on night shifts. Light mode works for daylight deliveries. Automatic switching respects your phone's settings. And your company's brand colours appear throughout, creating a cohesive, professional experience that's distinctly theirs.

---

## Key Benefits

- **Night Shift Eye Protection**: Dark mode reduces eye strain and blue light during late deliveries—your eyes stay fresher, you stay alert longer
- **AMOLED Battery Boost**: On modern phones, dark mode actually reduces battery drain—dark pixels use less power than light pixels, meaningful difference over a long shift
- **Automatic Smart Switching**: Set the app to follow your device's light/dark preference—switches automatically as you rotate, as the sun sets, as your device settings change
- **Consistent Theme Everywhere**: Every screen, menu, button, modal, and dialog respects your theme choice—no jarring light boxes popping up in dark mode
- **High Contrast for Real Visibility**: Text and controls remain sharp and readable in all conditions—no low-contrast problems, no "can I read this?" doubts
- **Your Company's Visual Identity**: Brand colour customisation means the app feels distinctly yours—not just white-labeled, but branded with actual company colours
- **Smooth, Polished Transitions**: Theme changes animate smoothly without flashing or reloading—the app feels premium and responsive
- **Personalisation Persists**: Your theme preference is remembered across every session, every device—set once, consistent experience forever

---

## How It Works

**1. Set Your Preference**
In settings, choose light mode, dark mode, or "System" to match your device's theme setting.

**2. Theme Applies Globally**
Every screen immediately adopts your selected theme—backgrounds, text, buttons, modals, everything.

**3. Automatic Updates**
If you select "System" mode and toggle your device between light and dark, the app switches automatically.

**4. Organisation Branding**
Administrators can configure brand colours that apply throughout the app, creating a customised experience for their team.

## Why Choose This Feature

Dark mode isn't just aesthetic—it's functional. Late-night deliveries with a bright white screen genuinely strain your eyes. Battery drains faster. A dark interface is more comfortable and helps you stay alert through night shifts.

For companies, branded themes create psychological connection. When drivers open the app, they see your company colours and feel they're using a professional tool built specifically for them—not a generic third-party system. Brand ownership increases. The app feels premium.

The automatic system linking demonstrates thoughtful design. When your device switches to dark mode at sunset, the app switches with it. No inconsistency. No manual adjustments. Everything feels interconnected and seamless—hallmark of a well-designed application.
