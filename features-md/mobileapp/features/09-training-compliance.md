# Training & Compliance
## Compliance stops being a pain—training in your pocket, safety checks automated, certificates managed for you

Compliance used to mean bureaucracy. You get sent to a training centre. You sit through hours of generic content. You get a certificate. Months later, you forget when it expires. Suddenly you're non-compliant. Your company is exposed to liability. You're scrambling to get recertified.

Our system makes compliance seamless. Training modules are short, focused, and done in your spare time. You take a vehicle walk-around before each shift—5 minutes, automated documentation, zero confusion about what was checked. Your company always knows who's compliant. You always know when certifications expire. The compliance machine runs smoothly instead of creating drama.

---

## Key Benefits

- **Compliance Happens Automatically, Not as a Burden**: Training delivered through the app during your spare time, vehicle inspections automated into your pre-shift routine—compliance feels like normal work, not bureaucracy
- **Never Scramble to Recertify**: Automatic expiry reminders give you weeks of notice before certifications lapse—you renew proactively instead of discovering you're non-compliant when needed
- **Vehicle Safety Stops Being a Surprise**: Pre-trip walk-around checklists catch problems before they become dangerous or costly—check tire pressure, lights, brakes, mirrors automatically documented before you leave
- **Instant Regulatory Proof**: All training completion and safety checks are automatically documented with timestamps and evidence—audits and insurance claims have perfect records instantly
- **Learn at Your Pace, Not Someone Else's**: Short, focused mobile modules mean you can do training between deliveries, at lunch, whenever—not waiting for scheduled training dates months away
- **Company Always Knows Compliance Status**: Your manager sees real-time compliance dashboard—no surprises, no drivers unexpectedly out of compliance, perfect visibility
- **Certificates Tracked, Never Forgotten**: System tracks expiry dates and sends reminders automatically—you never forget a renewal, your company never worries about liability exposure from lapsed certifications
- **Knowledge Actually Sticks**: Interactive learning, quizzes, and video content are more effective than PowerPoint slides—better training, better safety outcomes, better compliance

## How It Works

**1. View Your Training**
Open the training section to see mandatory courses, optional professional development, and upcoming certification renewals.

**2. Complete Courses**
Select a course and start learning. Progress is saved automatically, so you can stop and resume anytime. Modules include videos, documents, and quizzes.

**3. Walk-Around Checks**
Before each shift, open the vehicle inspection checklist. Walk around your vehicle checking tires, lights, mirrors, and brakes. Confirm each item, and submit the completed check.

**4. Track Progress**
Monitor your training completion percentage and certification status. Receive alerts when certificates are about to expire.

## Why Choose This Feature

Compliance is non-negotiable. But traditional compliance methods are painful: scheduled training you can't attend, paper records that get lost, certificate tracking that's manual and error-prone, sudden panics when you realize something expired months ago.

Our system embeds compliance into normal operations instead of creating a separate bureaucratic burden. Training is in your app, delivered in short bursts you can complete anytime. Vehicle safety checks are part of your pre-shift routine, not separate paperwork. Certificate tracking is automatic with proactive reminders. Everything is documented digitally, so audits become simple instead of chaotic.

**For your company**: Real-time compliance visibility. They see which drivers are fully certified, which are approaching expiry, and which have completed training. They can prove compliance to auditors instantly with perfect records. They eliminate the risk of someone being non-compliant without anyone noticing. Compliance shifts from a crisis management problem to a smooth, automated process.
