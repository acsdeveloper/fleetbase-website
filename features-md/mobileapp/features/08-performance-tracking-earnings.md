# Performance Tracking & Earnings
## Know exactly what you're earning, right now—every delivery, every bonus, every penny

Imagine finishing your shift and not knowing how much you earned. That's most gig economy workers' reality. Payment comes at the end of the month. You see a single number. No breakdown. No details. Questions go unanswered because nobody's around to ask.

Our system shows you earnings in real-time as you complete deliveries. You see bonuses, tips, deductions, everything broken down clearly. You know today's total. This week's total. This month's progress. If you see surge pricing coming next weekend, you can plan accordingly. If you're 20 deliveries away from a completion bonus, you know exactly how many more to take.

- **Earnings Predictions for Financial Peace**: See your month-to-date total and project where you'll land by month-end—better budgeting, financial confidence, no surprises
- **Settlement Status Always Clear**: Know exactly when your next payment arrives and what's in it—no "when's my money?" anxiety, no payment surprises

## How It Works

**1. Complete Deliveries**
As you complete deliveries, each earns payment based on your rates and any applicable bonuses or surge pricing.

**2. Track Earnings**
Open your wallet to see today's earnings, this week's total, and this month's accumulated income. View the breakdown by delivery type.

**3. Monitor Settlement**
Track when your earnings are processed, any deductions or adjustments, and exactly when the payment hits your account.

**4. Review History**
Access historical payment records, settlement statements, and year-to-date totals for personal accounting and tax planning.

## Why Choose This Feature

Financial clarity changes behaviour. When you know that surge pricing kicks in on Friday mornings, you schedule Friday mornings. When you see that bonuses apply to 25+ deliveries, you chase 25+. When you understand exactly what each delivery earns, you make strategic decisions about which jobs to take.

Traditional systems hide this. You get a paycheck at month-end with a single number. Zero transparency. If there's a deduction, you're confused. If your payment is delayed, you're stressed. You can't see patterns. You can't optimise. You're just hoping you earned enough.

Our system creates financial clarity. Real-time earnings let you see exactly what you're making as it happens. Detailed breakdowns show you what drives income. Historical data reveals patterns. And because you can see it all, you make smarter decisions about when and how hard to work. You're not working blind—you're optimising your time based on real data.
