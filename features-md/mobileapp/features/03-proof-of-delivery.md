## How It Works

**1. Complete the Delivery**
When you've delivered the package or completed the service, open the order and select the proof-of-delivery option.

**2. Capture Photo Evidence**
Use your device camera to photograph the delivery location, recipient, or package placement. The app automatically captures location metadata (GPS, timestamp, orientation).

**3. Review & Confirm**
Preview the photo to ensure it clearly shows the delivery. Add optional notes or customer signature if required.

**4. Upload & Archive**
The photo automatically uploads to secure cloud storage with full metadata intact. Your operations team immediately receives confirmation of completion.
