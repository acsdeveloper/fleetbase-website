## How It Works

**1. Receive & Review**
New orders appear instantly on your dashboard with complete delivery details, customer contact information, and special handling instructions.

**2. Accept or Manage**
Make informed decisions about which jobs to accept. Your workload stays manageable, and you maintain control over your schedule.

**3. Update & Track**
As you complete deliveries, update the order status in real-time. Track progress through each delivery, and get instant confirmation when jobs are completed.

**4. Sync & Confirm**
All updates automatically sync to the backend when you regain connectivity. No lost work, no manual re-entry—everything stays in perfect sync.
