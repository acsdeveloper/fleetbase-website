## Core Features

These essential features form the foundation of your delivery operations, handling orders, navigation, communication, and daily tasks.

### 1. [Real-Time Order Management](01-real-time-order-management.md)
Accept new jobs, view detailed delivery information, and track orders in real-time—all from your mobile device. Our intelligent queue system ensures you never miss a job, even when offline.

### 2. [GPS Navigation & Route Tracking](02-gps-navigation-route-tracking.md)
Navigate with confidence using real-time maps and turn-by-turn guidance. Built-in mapping with flexible integration to Google Maps, Apple Maps, or Waze.

### 3. [Proof of Delivery](03-proof-of-delivery.md)
Capture irrefutable delivery evidence with photos and location data. Automatic EXIF metadata extraction ensures compliance and reduces disputes.

### 4. [Real-Time Chat & Communication](04-real-time-chat-communication.md)
Stay connected with instant team messaging and organised channels. Direct communication eliminates delays and keeps conversations focused.

### 5. [Expense & Receipt Management](05-expense-receipt-management.md)
Log expenses effortlessly with AI-powered receipt recognition. No more manual data entry—photograph receipts and let machine learning extract the details.

### 6. [Issue Reporting](06-issue-reporting.md)
Report operational problems instantly with categorised tracking. Real-time escalation ensures problems get immediate attention from the right teams.

### 7. [Shift & Leave Management](07-shift-leave-management.md)
Manage your schedule with flexible requests and clear visibility. View assigned shifts, request time off, and track leave approvals instantly.

### 8. [Performance Tracking & Earnings](08-performance-tracking-earnings.md)
Monitor your earnings in real-time with complete financial transparency. View daily breakdown, payment history, and settlement status.

### 9. [Training & Compliance](09-training-compliance.md)
Stay compliant with accessible training and vehicle inspection checklists. Complete mandatory courses and pre-trip checks directly in the app.

### 10. [Driver Profile & Documents](10-driver-profile-documents.md)
Centralise all your credentials and documentation in one secure location. Manage your profile, upload certifications, and track expiry dates.

## Advanced Capabilities

These powerful features unlock efficiency gains and operational insights, enabling sophisticated delivery and business management.

### 11. [Offline-First Architecture](11-offline-first-architecture.md)
Work anywhere with automatic syncing when connectivity returns. Never lose work due to connectivity issues—everything queues and syncs seamlessly.

### 12. [AI-Powered Receipt Recognition](12-ai-receipt-recognition.md)
Eliminate manual expense entry with intelligent receipt detection. Machine learning automatically extracts vendor, amount, and date information.

### 13. [Background Location Tracking](13-background-location-tracking.md)
Maintain precise GPS tracking during deliveries without draining battery. Continuous monitoring with intelligent battery optimisation and geofencing.

### 14. [Push Notifications & Real-Time Updates](14-push-notifications-real-time-updates.md)
Stay instantly informed with live order changes and urgent alerts. WebSocket integration ensures you're always in sync with operations.

### 15. [Biometric & Secure Authentication](15-biometric-secure-authentication.md)
Login securely with multi-method authentication and device verification. Phone and email OTP verification protect your account without password hassles.

### 16. [Internationalisation & Localisation](16-internationalisation-localisation.md)
Work in your language with support for multiple regions and currencies. Complete multi-language support with persistent preferences and real-time currency conversion.

### 17. [Dark Mode & Customisable Themes](17-dark-mode-customisable-themes.md)
Protect your eyes with adaptive theme support and brand-aligned customisation. Dark mode reduces eye strain; organisational branding creates consistency.

### 18. [Responsive Mobile UI](18-responsive-mobile-ui.md)
Fast, intuitive interface designed for real-world field operations. Mobile-first design adapts to any screen size with smooth animations and reliable performance.
