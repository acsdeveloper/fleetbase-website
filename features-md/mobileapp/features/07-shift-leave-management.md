# Shift & Leave Management
## Know your schedule perfectly—plan personal time with zero confusion or back-and-forth

Uncertainty about your schedule creates stress. You don't know if next weekend is free. You want time off but don't know how to ask. You're unsure if a shift swap got approved. These small confusions add up to poor work-life balance and frustration.

Our system gives you complete schedule clarity. You see your shifts for weeks ahead. You request time off in 10 seconds with instant approval status. You swap shifts with colleagues directly. No email threads. No "did they get my message?" anxiety. Everything is clear and confirmed.

---

## Key Benefits

- **Schedule Certainty Reduces Stress**: See your shifts weeks in advance with dates, times, and locations—plan your personal life confidently instead of being constantly uncertain
- **Instant Approval, No Waiting Games**: Request leave and see approval status immediately—no "did they see my email?" anxiety, no following up three days later, no uncertainty

- **Self-Service Management, No Manager Juggling**: Stop bothering your manager with "how many days off do I have?" questions—all answers are in the app instantly

## How It Works

**1. View Schedule**
Open the schedule view to see your assigned shifts for the next week, month, or custom period. Each shift shows date, time, location, and assigned vehicle.

**2. Request Time Off**
Select a date on the calendar and tap "Request Leave." Choose your reason (vacation, sick leave, personal), add optional notes, and submit instantly.

**3. Track Approval**
Your request goes to management for approval. Check the status anytime—pending approval, approved, or needs more information.

**4. Receive Reminders**
Get notifications about your upcoming shifts. Know exactly when and where you need to be to start your delivery route.

## Why Choose This Feature

Traditional schedule management is chaos. Drivers call their manager about time off. The manager checks the schedule (which is still in email or a spreadsheet), approves verbally, and then forgets to update anything. Days later, there's confusion about who's actually working. Someone thought they had Tuesday off and finds out they're expected in. Shift swaps happen verbally and never get recorded.

Our system creates a single source of truth. You see your schedule weeks in advance. You request time off and get instant approval. You swap shifts directly with colleagues through the app. Everything is documented. Everything is clear.

**For your company**: Clear schedules mean better coverage planning. They can see leave requests instantly and plan accordingly. Shift swaps are documented for audit purposes. And drivers are happier because the anxiety and confusion about scheduling disappears.
