# Driver Profile & Documents
## Your entire portfolio in your pocket—always ready to prove your credentials

Important documents used to live in filing cabinets. You'd search frantically for your insurance certificate or license copy when your company needed them. Documents got lost. Certifications went missing. You couldn't prove you were qualified when you needed to.

Now everything's in your phone. Your license, insurance, permits, certifications—all digitally organized, all with automatic expiry tracking. Your company needs proof you're insured? Done in a tap. Need to renew a license? The app reminds you weeks in advance. You're never scrambling for documents again.

---

## Key Benefits

- **Your Credentials Always Ready**: Every document you need is in your phone, instantly accessible—no searching filing cabinets or hunting through drawers when your company or insurance companies ask for proof
- **Never Miss a Renewal**: Automatic expiry tracking reminds you weeks in advance when insurance, licenses, or certifications are due for renewal—no more surprises discovering something lapsed
- **Instant Company Verification**: Instead of scanning documents and emailing them, your company verifies your credentials in seconds by accessing the app—faster onboarding, no bureaucratic delays
- **Complete Privacy Control**: You decide who sees what—your company sees your license and insurance, but not your personal financial documents—your documents, your control
- **Backup Against Loss**: Every document is backed up securely—even if you lose your phone, your documents are safe and recoverable in minutes
- **Perfect Audit Trail**: All documents are digitally stored with timestamps showing when they were uploaded and when they expire—perfect for insurance investigations or compliance audits
- **Emergency Contact Management**: Your emergency contacts are stored securely and instantly accessible to medical personnel or your employer if something happens while you're working
- **Document History Tracking**: Keep multiple versions of documents and see the history—important if a document was lost or corrupted and you need to prove you had valid credentials at a specific time

---

## How It Works

**1. Complete Your Profile**
Set up your driver profile with personal information, emergency contacts, and driver preferences.

**2. Upload Documents**
Select each document type—driver's license, insurance certificate, certifications—and upload clear photos or scans.

**3. Track Expiries**
The system tracks expiry dates on all documents. You get reminders before documents expire so you can renew proactively.

**4. Share When Needed**
When your company needs to verify credentials, documents are instantly available instead of searching for paperwork.
