# Background Location Tracking
## Silent, efficient, always on—GPS tracking that doesn't kill your battery or slow your phone

Your phone tracks your location all day. Most apps either kill your battery doing it or don't track accurately enough to matter. Our system does both: precise continuous GPS tracking AND intelligent battery optimization. You don't think about it. It just works in the background.

Geofencing means you don't manually confirm arrivals at each stop—the system knows automatically when you've arrived. Your operations team sees exactly where you are in real-time. And because we route everything efficiently, your phone battery stays healthy all day.

## Key Benefits

- **Invisible Efficiency**: Background tracking works silently without slowing your phone or killing battery—precise location data with zero impact on your device
- **Automatic Arrival Detection Eliminates Manual Confirmations**: Geofencing knows when you've arrived at delivery locations—no tapping buttons to confirm, just automatic detection
- **Battery Stays Healthy All Shift**: Intelligent tracking adjusts frequency based on your speed and proximity to deliveries—aggressive when it matters, conservative when it doesn't—all-day tracking without draining power
- **Complete Route Audit Trail**: Every route is logged with precise GPS breadcrumbs—indisputable proof of where you've been, when you've been there, and how long you stayed
- **Real-Time Team Visibility Without Excessive Data Usage**: Your operations team sees live location updates without you burning through data allowance—efficient data transmission means lower costs
- **Privacy Remains Yours**: Location data is encrypted and accessible only to authorised people in your company—not visible to customers, not exposed online, not sold to third parties
- **Works Perfectly on Both Platforms**: iOS and Android both get optimised tracking for their specific platform—no compromises on either device
- **Flexible Geofencing for Real Situations**: Adjust the arrival radius from 50 to 500 metres depending on customer locations—urban apartment buildings need tight accuracy, suburban houses need wider margins

## How It Works

**1. Start Your Shift**
Enable background location tracking when you begin your deliveries. Permission requests are handled automatically.

**2. Deliver Naturally**
As you drive and deliver, the system tracks your location continuously in the background. You don't think about it—it just works.

**3. Arrive at Stop**
Geofencing automatically detects your arrival at delivery locations. The app can trigger proof-of-delivery prompts or order status updates automatically.

**4. Complete Route**
Your entire route is logged with GPS precision. Location history provides evidence of where you've been and when.

## Why Choose This Feature

Location tracking is essential, but traditional GPS tracking is a battery killer. Run it all day and your phone dies mid-afternoon. Most drivers disable it after a few days because the battery drain is unacceptable.

Our system is different. Intelligent sampling means we track precisely when it matters—when you're driving or at a delivery location. When you're stationary, we reduce tracking to save power. All-day coverage, healthy battery. You don't have to choose between tracking and keeping your phone alive.

Geofencing adds a layer of convenience. When you arrive at a delivery location, the system knows automatically. No need to manually confirm arrival or update status—it happens automatically based on your location. For your operations team, live location updates mean real visibility into where work is happening and proof it's been completed. Customers get tracking transparency. Your company gets evidence of completion. You get a system that works smoothly without you thinking about it.
