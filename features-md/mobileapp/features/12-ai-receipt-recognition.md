# AI-Powered Receipt Recognition
## One photo, instant extraction—machine learning does the tedious data typing for you

You've got a stack of receipts, a pen, and a spreadsheet. Or worse, a form you have to fill out manually. Five minutes per receipt, typing in information that's already printed. This is how most driver expense reports still work—pure tedium.

Our AI-powered receipt recognition changes this completely. You photograph a receipt. Done. The machine learning system instantly extracts vendor, amount, date, and category. Usually it's 100% accurate—you just review and submit. When it's uncertain, it tells you which fields need review. Batch processing means you can photograph 10 receipts and have them all extracted and ready to submit in 2 minutes instead of 50.

## Key Benefits

- **Minutes of Manual Work Becomes 10 Seconds**: Photograph the receipt, AI extracts everything, you verify and submit—transaction that took 5 minutes is done in 10 seconds

## How It Works

**1. Capture Receipt Photo**
Photograph the receipt with your phone's camera. The system automatically detects the receipt in the image frame.

**2. AI Analysis**
Machine learning instantly recognises the receipt format, detects text, and begins extracting key data fields.

**3. Extraction & Validation**
The system extracts vendor name, total amount, transaction date, and payment method. It validates data quality and flags uncertain extractions.

**4. Review & Confirm**
You review the extracted data in a simple form. Make corrections if needed (usually unnecessary), then submit.
