## Core Features (11 Pages)

Essential features your drivers use daily for delivery operations.

### 0. [Driver Operations](00-driver-operations.md)
Complete mobile delivery management—all driver operations tools consolidated into one intuitive app for order acceptance, navigation, and completion tracking.

### 1. [Real-Time Order Management](01-real-time-order-management.md)
Accept new jobs instantly, view detailed delivery information, and track orders throughout the lifecycle with offline queue support for seamless operations anywhere.

### 2. [GPS Navigation & Route Tracking](02-gps-navigation-route-tracking.md)
Navigate with confidence using real-time maps with flexible integration to Google Maps, Apple Maps, or Waze for turn-by-turn guidance and route optimisation.

### 3. [Proof of Delivery](03-proof-of-delivery.md)
Capture irrefutable delivery evidence with photos and automatic GPS metadata extraction for location verification and dispute resolution.

### 4. [Real-Time Chat & Communication](04-real-time-chat-communication.md)
Stay connected with instant team messaging and organised channels—direct communication with operations teams eliminates phone calls and coordination delays.

### 5. [Expense & Receipt Management](05-expense-receipt-management.md)
Log expenses effortlessly with AI-powered receipt recognition—photograph receipts and let machine learning automatically extract vendor, amount, and date information.

### 6. [Issue Reporting](06-issue-reporting.md)
Report operational problems instantly from the field with automatic categorisation and real-time escalation to the appropriate teams for immediate response.

### 7. [Shift & Leave Management](07-shift-leave-management.md)
Manage your schedule with flexible leave requests and clear visibility—view shifts, request time off with calendar integration, and track approval status.

### 8. [Performance Tracking & Earnings](08-performance-tracking-earnings.md)
Monitor earnings in real-time with complete financial transparency—view daily breakdown, payment history, and settlement status with multi-currency support.

### 9. [Training & Compliance](09-training-compliance.md)
Stay compliant with accessible training modules and pre-trip vehicle checklists—complete mandatory courses and safety checks directly in the app.

### 10. [Driver Profile & Documents](10-driver-profile-documents.md)
Centralise all credentials and documentation—manage your profile, upload certifications, and track expiry dates in one secure location.

## Advanced Capabilities (8 Pages)

Powerful features enabling efficiency, reliability, and advanced operations.

### 11. [Offline-First Architecture](11-offline-first-architecture.md)
Work anywhere without losing progress—automatically queue actions when offline, then sync everything seamlessly when connectivity is restored.

### 12. [AI-Powered Receipt Recognition](12-ai-receipt-recognition.md)
Eliminate manual expense entry with intelligent receipt detection—machine learning automatically detects receipts in photos and extracts key data fields.

### 13. [Background Location Tracking](13-background-location-tracking.md)
Maintain precise GPS tracking without draining battery—intelligent sampling adjusts frequency based on movement, with geofencing for automatic arrival detection.

### 14. [Push Notifications & Real-Time Updates](14-push-notifications-real-time-updates.md)
Stay instantly informed with live order changes and urgent alerts—WebSocket integration ensures you're always in sync with operations.

### 15. [Biometric & Secure Authentication](15-biometric-secure-authentication.md)
Login securely with multi-method authentication—phone and email OTP verification without password hassles, plus secure session management.

### 16. [Internationalisation & Localisation](16-internationalisation-localisation.md)
Work in your language across multiple regions—complete multi-language support with persistent preferences and real-time currency conversion.

### 17. [Dark Mode & Customisable Themes](17-dark-mode-customisable-themes.md)
Protect your eyes with adaptive theme support and brand-aligned customisation—dark mode reduces eye strain with organisational branding consistency.

### 18. [Responsive Mobile UI](18-responsive-mobile-ui.md)
Experience fast, intuitive interface designed for fieldwork—mobile-first design adapts to any screen size with smooth animations and reliable performance.

## Integration Ecosystem (7 Pages)

Technology integrations and APIs that power the platform.

### 21. [Google Maps & Navigation APIs](21-google-maps-navigation-apis.md)

### 22. [ML Kit Text Recognition](22-ml-kit-text-recognition.md)

### 25. [Image Processing & Camera Integration](25-image-processing.md)

### 26. [Calendar & Scheduling Integration](26-calendar-scheduling.md)

### 27. [Geolocation Services](27-geolocation-services.md)
