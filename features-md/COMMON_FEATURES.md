# Common Features: Web Dashboard & Mobile App

Features that exist on **both** the FleetYes Dashboard (web) and FleetYes Navigator (mobile).

---

## 1. Order/Trip Management
- **Dashboard:** Schedule, dispatch, and track trips end-to-end
- **Mobile:** Accept or reject jobs instantly; track order status from assignment to completion
- **Common capability:** View, manage, and track delivery/trip assignments in real-time

## 2. Real-Time Order/Job Notifications & Alerts
- **Dashboard:** Contextual alerts (unassigned trips, drivers at rest limit, vehicles due for inspection)
- **Mobile:** Instant alerts for new jobs and cancellations via push notifications
- **Common capability:** Keep users informed of urgent operational changes instantly

## 3. Expense & Receipt Management
- **Dashboard:** Record fuel purchases, expenses, and receipts with OCR; generate reports
- **Mobile:** Log expenses and capture receipts with AI-powered receipt recognition
- **Common capability:** Capture, categorize, and track expenses with receipt images and automatic data extraction

## 4. Compliance & Vehicle Inspection
- **Dashboard:** Walkaround & vehicle inspection with photo-based checks before and after trips
- **Mobile:** Complete mandatory safety checks before each shift with vehicle walk-around checklists
- **Common capability:** Document vehicle condition through photos and checklists for audit trails

## 5. Driver Documents & Certifications
- **Dashboard:** Staff training & compliance documents library; driver CPC tracking
- **Mobile:** Access training modules; manage driver documents, licenses, permits, and certifications
- **Common capability:** Centralized storage and tracking of driver qualifications and training records

## 6. Leave & Absence Management
- **Dashboard:** Record and approve driver leave; flag calendar conflicts
- **Mobile:** View shifts and request time off with calendar integration and approval tracking
- **Common capability:** Manage driver availability through leave requests and scheduling

## 7. Multi-Language & Dark Mode Support
- **Dashboard:** Available in multiple languages with automatic dark theme
- **Mobile:** Multi-language support and dark mode adapt to user preference
- **Common capability:** Internationalization and theme customization for accessibility

## 8. Real-Time Communication & Updates
- **Dashboard:** Operational alerts and real-time dashboard updates
- **Mobile:** Real-time message updates via WebSocket; team messaging and communication
- **Common capability:** Keep teams synchronized with instant notifications and live updates

## 9. GPS Location Tracking
- **Dashboard:** Real-time dashboard shows trips, drivers, and vehicles; monitoring capability
- **Mobile:** Real-time GPS tracking with background geolocation for operations teams to monitor driver location
- **Common capability:** Monitor driver/vehicle positions during deliveries and trips

---

**Summary:** Both platforms share **9 core operational capabilities** spanning orders, expenses, compliance, communications, and tracking. This alignment ensures consistency in the user experience between managers (web) and drivers (mobile).
