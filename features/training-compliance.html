How It Works

1

View Your Training

Open the training section to see mandatory courses, optional professional development, and upcoming certification renewals.

2

Complete Courses

Select a course and start learning. Progress is saved automatically, so you can stop and resume anytime. Modules include videos, documents, and quizzes.

3

Walk-Around Checks

Before each shift, open the vehicle inspection checklist. Walk around your vehicle checking tires, lights, mirrors, and brakes. Confirm each item, and submit the completed check.

4

Track Progress

Monitor your training completion percentage and certification status. Receive alerts when certificates are about to expire.

Why Choose Training & Compliance

Training compliance isn't optional-it's essential for safety, insurance, and legal compliance. But traditional training methods (in-person sessions, off-site courses) take drivers away from deliveries and cost money.

Our mobile training platform brings compliance training to your pocket. Required courses are accessible whenever you have downtime. Walk-around checklists take 5 minutes and catch vehicle problems before they become safety issues.

For your company, every completed training is automatically documented. You have proof of compliance for insurance audits. You can see which drivers need refresher training. When regulations change, you can push updated courses to everyone immediately.
