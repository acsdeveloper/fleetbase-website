All Features

Comprehensive transport management solutions designed for your business. Explore our complete suite of tools and capabilities to streamline operations, enhance efficiency, and deliver exceptional logistics performance.

Driver Operations

Complete order and delivery management from your mobile device. Accept jobs, navigate routes, and complete deliveries efficiently.

Real-Time Order Management

Instantly receive new orders, accept or reject jobs, and track order status throughout the delivery lifecycle with offline queue support.

GPS Navigation & Route Tracking

Built-in maps with real-time GPS tracking, route visualisation, and turn-by-turn navigation integrated with Google Maps, Apple Maps, and Waze.

Proof of Delivery

Capture photo evidence of completed deliveries with automatic EXIF metadata extraction for location confirmation and image optimisation.

Real-Time Chat & Communication

Team messaging system with channel organisation enabling drivers to communicate instantly with operations teams with persistent message history.

Expense & Receipt Management

Log expenses with automatic categorisation. AI-powered receipt detection automatically extracts vendor, amount, and date information from photos.

Issue Reporting

Report operational issues, vehicle problems, or customer concerns directly from the app with real-time resolution status tracking.

Shift & Leave Management

View assigned shifts, request time off with calendar integration, and track leave approval status with upcoming schedule visibility.

Performance Tracking & Earnings

Monitor daily earnings breakdown, view payment history, track settlement status with multi-currency support and real-time conversion rates.

Training & Compliance

Access mandatory and elective training modules, track completion progress, and perform pre-trip vehicle walk-around checks for regulatory compliance.

Driver Profile & Documents

Manage your driver profile, upload identification documents, and store important certifications with organised, accessible documentation.

Offline-First Architecture

Seamlessly work offline with automatic queuing of actions. All pending orders and submissions sync automatically when connectivity is restored.

AI-Powered Receipt Recognition

Intelligent machine learning automatically detects receipts, extracts text using OCR, and parses key data fields eliminating manual entry.

Background Location Tracking

Continuous GPS tracking during deliveries with efficient battery usage. Geofencing support for automatic arrival detection at delivery locations.

Push Notifications & Real-Time Updates

Receive instant alerts for new orders, cancellations, and urgent messages with WebSocket integration for live data synchronisation.

Multi-Instance Deep Linking

Dynamic API endpoint configuration via deep links supporting multiple Fleetbase instances enabling one app to work with different backend systems.

Biometric & Secure Authentication

Multi-method login with phone and email OTP verification with secure session management and device sync capabilities for data protection.

Internationalisation & Localisation

Support for multiple languages and regional preferences with language selection that persists across sessions for seamless global operations.

Dark Mode & Customisable Themes

Complete dark mode implementation with automatic theme detection and adaptive UI respecting device theme preferences with customisable colour schemes.

Responsive Mobile UI

Mobile-first design adapting to various screen sizes with smooth animations, loading states, error handling, and reusable component library.

Fleetbase SDK

Complete backend integration for orders, driver management, and operations data with seamless connectivity to your Fleetbase instance.

Google Maps & Navigation APIs

Real-time maps, directions, and geocoding with direct launch to Google Maps, Apple Maps, or Waze for flexible navigation options.

ML Kit Text Recognition

On-device OCR for receipt detection and text extraction with no external API calls required for enhanced privacy and security.

Push Notification Services

iOS and Android push notification delivery with Rich Notification support enabling instant alerts and real-time communication.

WebSocket (SocketCluster)

Real-time event streaming for orders, messages, and live updates enabling instantaneous data synchronisation across all operations.

Image Processing

Vision Camera (iOS), Image Picker, Image Resizer for optimised photo handling with EXIF data extraction for metadata verification.

Calendar & Scheduling

Date picker integration for leave requests and scheduling enabling seamless calendar management and shift planning capabilities.

Geolocation Services

Precise GPS tracking with background location support and battery optimisation ensuring continuous tracking during deliveries efficiently.
